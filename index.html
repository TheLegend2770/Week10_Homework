History of Cheese

Cheese making can be traced back almost 10,000 years ago, with evidence of countries developing cheese in their own ways. All countries have cheeses unique to their regions. The famous Green County Cheese factory is known to produce 200 different types of cheese. (National Historic Cheese Making Center, https://nationalhistoriccheesemakingcenter.org/history-of-cheese/)

Cheese is a staple food used around the world. It's a food that even has competitions based on it, in which countries compete to decide who produces the best cheese. Are there some countires that are better at making cheeses than others, and if so what are the possible reasons for this cheese making predisposition.
